ABOUT MATATUU

MATATUU is a web app that avails information on;

MATATUU is a smart business application model targeting the public transport sector in Kenya. Primarily, the project shall focus on solving consumer-related issues facing the sector today. It's aim is to improve public transport services across the nation. This idea was brought about because several kenyans are often late for work this is because of the massive amount of traffic that emerges in Kenya. The solution to this was to create matatuu to enable to avoid the clustering of so many people at one place to enable easy movement and for the users of the matatuu to be able to get where they wish to be fast and effectively.
